Flowering Dates of the Cherry Blossoms in Japan

I recently created a captivating connected scatterplot showcasing the bloom rate of cherry blossoms in Japan over the years. This unique visualization depicts the relationship between the year (on the x-axis) and the bloom date (e.g., April 15) on the y-axis. To add a visually appealing touch.

Additionally, to enhance the overall presentation, I incorporated a background image onto the webpage. The chosen background image resonates with the theme of the report. I used the Deneb plugin to craft an interactive line chart with Vega-Lite. This chart illustrates the blooming years and dates of cherry blossoms. I customized the data points with flower icons to add appeal to the visualization.
